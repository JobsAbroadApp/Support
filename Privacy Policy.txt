We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to delete your account. If you wish to delete the account, you can do so by deleting your account. The Delete Account option is present in the Setting Tab

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the application, please leave a comment in the Play Store reviews. We will address your concerns, consider your suggestions, and work on improvements to provide better job information and support your career. Thank you for your support!
